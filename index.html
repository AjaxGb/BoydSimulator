BOYD'S CONSPIRACY SIM

https://github.com/AjaxGb/BoydSimulator

  • This is a non-profit personal project to recreate the randomly generated ramblings of Boyd Cooper from the game Psychonauts. All voice acting and writing comes from the game itself. If you do not own Psychonauts, you should! It's a great game.
